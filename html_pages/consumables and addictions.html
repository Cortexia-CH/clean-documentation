Iqos

Iqos look like cigarette but a few features makes us able to differenciate them:
They are fully white.
They don't burn, just heat, so they are bigger than cigarette butts but smaller than entire cigarettes.
They are thiner than regular cigarettes
They keep a nice shape (regular, never flat) because they are more solid than cigarettes
